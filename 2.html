Alumni Contributions: Powering the Future

At the Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology (RGIPT), our alumni are more than just former students—they are mentors, trailblazers, and torchbearers of the Institute's vision as well as the Institute’s strongest ambassadors and enduring pillars of support. Over the years, our graduates have not only made their mark across industries and academia but have also remained deeply connected to their alma mater.
As RGIPT continues to grow as a hub of excellence in energy education and research, the unwavering support of its alumni has become instrumental in shaping its future. Your contribution—whether in the form of time, expertise, mentorship, or financial support—plays a pivotal role in shaping the future of RGIPT. It empowers us to enhance academic excellence, support meritorious and underprivileged students, foster innovation and entrepreneurship, upgrade research infrastructure, and build a vibrant and inclusive campus ecosystem.
We are proud to announce a series of prestigious student awards, made possible through the generous contributions of our alumni and well-wishers. These awards aim to recognize and celebrate outstanding achievements in academics, research, innovation, leadership, social service, sustainability, sports, music, and campus life. Starting from the academic year 2025–26, the following honors will be instituted annually.
Every act of giving, regardless of size, contributes meaningfully to RGIPT’s journey towards becoming a world-class institution in energy education and research. We invite you to explore the various ways you can give back and make a lasting impact on the lives of current and future generations of RGIPTians.

About the RGIPT Alumni Association

Our Mission

To foster a lifelong, mutually beneficial relationship between Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology and its global community of alumni. We aim to connect alumni to the institute and to each other through a wealth of programmes, services, and communications.

Our Vision

To be a dynamic, member-centric organization that is recognized for its valuable contributions to the academic and professional development of RGIPT students and alumni, and to the continued advancement of the institute's legacy of excellence in the energy sector.

The Journey of RGIPT

2007

Establishment

Established by the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas through an Act of Parliament, RGIPT is declared an "Institute of National Importance".

2008

First Academic Session

The first batch of undergraduate students is admitted, marking the beginning of academic activities and a new era in energy education.

2016

Expansion to Bengaluru

The Energy Institute is established in Bengaluru to cater to the growing needs of the energy sector with specialized programs.

2017

New Campus in Assam

The Assam Energy Institute is established in Sivasagar, expanding RGIPT's footprint and commitment to the nation's energy security.

Present Day

A Leader in Energy Education

RGIPT continues to grow, producing world-class professionals and contributing to research and innovation across the entire energy value chain.

What We Do

  • Organize annual alumni meets, chapter events, and reunions.
  • Facilitate mentorship programs connecting students with experienced alumni.
  • Promote networking and professional development opportunities.
  • Manage alumni contribution funds for scholarships and institute development.
  • Publish newsletters and magazines to keep alumni informed.

A Message from the Dean

Dear Alumni,

I hope this message finds you all in good health and high spirits. As the Dean of Alumni Affairs, it is my great pleasure to extend a warm welcome to each one of you. Our alumni community is the heartbeat of our institution, serving as a testament to the transformative power of education and the lasting impact it has on individuals and society as a whole. We take immense pride in the accomplishments and contributions of our alumni, who have gone on to excel in various fields, making a difference in their respective professions and communities.

Through this dedicated web page and alma connect portal, we aim to foster a strong bond between the Institute and its esteemed alumni. It serves as a platform for you to stay connected, share your achievements, and engage in meaningful interactions with your fellow alumni, faculty, and current students. Whether you graduated recently or ago, we believe in nurturing lifelong relationships with our alumni. We want to provide you with opportunities to network, collaborate, and continue your personal and professional growth. Our alumni events, reunions, and special gatherings are designed to rekindle old friendships and forge new ones, creating a vibrant and supportive network that extends far beyond the boundaries of our campus.

- Dr. Satish Kumar Sinha
